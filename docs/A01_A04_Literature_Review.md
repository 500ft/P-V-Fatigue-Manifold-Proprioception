# Annotated Literature Base — P-V Hysteresis Fatigue Leading Indicator × Pressure-Only Proprioception in Shared-Manifold Soft Grippers

Compiled 2026-06-18 via a 6-agent deep-research sweep, followed by a metadata cleanup pass against Crossref, arXiv, and Google Patents. "PRESSURE-ONLY" vs "ADDED-SENSOR" tags on proprioception papers prevent the misattribution that affected Draft 1.

Role legend: **F** = foundational · **CPA** = closest prior art · **M** = methodological · **S** = supporting.

## 1. Soft pneumatic actuator fatigue, lifetime & P-V / viscoelastic characterization

- **Mosadegh et al. 2014** — "Pneumatic Networks for Soft Robotics that Actuate Rapidly," *Adv. Funct. Mater.* 24(15):2163–2170. DOI 10.1002/adfm.201303288. **F / CPA.** Defines the PneuNet class — and **assesses fatigue by measuring P-V hysteresis curves before vs. after >10⁶ cycles.** This is direct precedent that P-V hysteresis tracks fatigue; your contribution must be the *cycle-resolved leading-indicator* framing, not the use of P-V per se.
- **Mosadegh, Shepherd, Whitesides 2020** — "Low strain pneumatic networks for soft robots," US Patent **US10639801B2** (priority 2013-08-20; granted 2020-05-05). **CPA / patent.** The downloaded Google Patents PDF confirms durability claims for >10,000, >200,000, and >1,000,000 cycles without failure at >1–2 Hz; the Google Patents text also states that fatigue was assessed by measuring P-V hysteresis before and after cyclic testing at 2 Hz for 10⁴, 2×10⁵, and 10⁶ complete-actuation cycles, with curves reportedly not changing substantially. This strengthens the boundary: your claim is not "P-V for fatigue," but cycle-resolved prognostic lead time.
- **Libby et al. 2023** — "What Happens When Pneu-Net Soft Robotic Actuators Get Fatigued?" *ISMR 2023*, DOI 10.1109/ISMR57123.2023.10130227 (arXiv:2212.03420). **CPA.** Cyclic fatigue degrades FEM-predicted behavior (96%→80%); reports that **P-V hysteresis shifts with fatigue.** Same lab (Atashzar/Rizzo, NYU) you're adjacent to. The seed your thesis operationalizes.
- **Libby et al. 2023** — "How Does the Inner Geometry of Soft Actuators Modulate the Dynamic and Hysteretic Response?" arXiv:2308.04722. **F.** Chamber geometry programs the hysteresis/dynamics trade-off → loop shape carries mechanical-state information.
- **Wong, Luo, Scharff 2026** — "Durability of Soft Pneumatic Actuators: A Review and Benchmarking Protocol," *Advanced Robotics Research*, article e202500172. DOI 10.1002/adrr.202500172. **CPA.** Current durability reference frame (cycles-to-failure). Position your leading indicator against this *lagging* metric.
- **Torzini et al. 2024** — "Characterization of fatigue behavior of 3D printed pneumatic fluidic elastomer actuators," *Int. J. Adv. Manuf. Technol.* 134:2725–2736. DOI 10.1007/s00170-024-14216-0. **M.** Inflate/deflate-to-failure protocol; cycles-to-failure baselines; microtears before rupture.
- **Mars & Fatemi 2002** — "A literature survey on fatigue analysis approaches for rubber," *Int. J. Fatigue* 24(9):949–961. DOI 10.1016/S0142-1123(02)00008-7. **F.** Canonical elastomer-fatigue mechanics (crack nucleation vs. growth, energy-based parameters). Grounds your framing beyond soft-robotics-only literature.
- **de la Morena, Ramos, Vázquez 2025** — "Hysteresis Modeling of Soft Pneumatic Actuators: An Experimental Review," *Actuators* 14(7):321. DOI 10.3390/act14070321. **M / CPA.** Characterizes the P-V loop but treats it as a *control nuisance*, not a health signal — defines the unoccupied space your thesis fills. Quasi-static excitation to isolate hysteresis.
- **Lavazza, Contino, Marano 2023** — "Strain rate, temperature and deformation state effect on Ecoflex 00-50 silicone mechanical behaviour," *Mech. of Materials* 178:104560. DOI 10.1016/j.mechmat.2023.104560. **F.** Mullins/stress-softening stabilizes after ~5 cycles (<5% thereafter). **Critical confounder control:** your baseline must be taken post-Mullins.
- **Liao et al. 2021** — "On the stress recovery behaviour of Ecoflex silicone rubbers," *Int. J. Mech. Sci.* 206:106624. DOI 10.1016/j.ijmecsci.2021.106624. **F.** Stress softening is partly *recoverable* at rest — you must separate reversible recovery from irreversible fatigue in the loop signal.
- **Marechal et al. 2021** — "Toward a Common Framework and Database of Materials for Soft Robotics," *Soft Robotics* 8(3):284–297. DOI 10.1089/soro.2019.0115. **S.** Elastomer property database (Dragon Skin/Ecoflex).
- **Case, White, Kramer 2015** — "Soft Material Characterization for Robotic Applications," *Soft Robotics* 2(2):80–87. DOI 10.1089/soro.2015.0002. **F.** Origin of "characterize elastomer time-dependence for modeling."
- **Kurata et al. 2025** — "Dynamic response characterization of soft fluidic actuators via dielectric elastomer sensors," *Measurement* 246:116616. DOI 10.1016/j.measurement.2024.116616. **S.** Alternative in-situ deformation-sensing modality.

## 2. Health monitoring / prognostics & transferable early-warning methodology

- **Scheffer et al. 2009** — "Early-warning signals for critical transitions," *Nature* 461:53–59. DOI 10.1038/nature08227. **F/M.** Generic precursors (rising variance, critical slowing down) precede tipping points → licenses treating drifting loop statistics as an early warning.
- **Lei et al. 2018** — "Machinery health prognostics: A systematic review from data acquisition to RUL prediction," *MSSP* 104:799–834. DOI 10.1016/j.ymssp.2017.11.016. **M.** The 4-stage prognostics pipeline + health-indicator quality criteria (monotonicity, trendability, prognosability) you'll evaluate your P-V feature against.
- **Haghshenas, Jang, Khonsari 2021** — "On the intrinsic dissipation and fracture fatigue entropy of metals," *Mech. of Materials* 155:103734. DOI 10.1016/j.mechmat.2020.103734. **M.** Per-cycle hysteresis-loop **area = dissipated energy = damage proxy** — the exact mechanistic logic for your loop-area feature, in metals.
- **Guo, Ma, Dong, Liang, Hu 2021** — "A novel health indicator based on hysteresis loop for health prediction of flight control systems," *Measurement* 186:110076. DOI 10.1016/j.measurement.2021.110076. **CPA (cross-domain).** Explicit precedent for "hysteresis-loop shape as a health/leading indicator."
- **Galanopoulos et al. 2023** — "Acoustic emission-based remaining useful life prognosis of aeronautical structures…," *Eng. Structures* 290:116391. DOI 10.1016/j.engstruct.2023.116391. **M.** Operational-signal feature → RUL template; AE is to composites what loop-shape is to your actuators.
- **Sugiyama et al. 2025** — "Versatile graceful degradation framework for bio-inspired proprioception with redundant soft sensors," *Front. Robot. AI* 11:1504651. DOI 10.3389/frobt.2024.1504651. **CPA.** Solves the *non-unique-mapping* problem (degraded reading mimics a different healthy state) via stochastic-LSTM fault detection — the closest analogue to your "is the signal aging or is the state changing?" problem.
- **López-Díaz et al. 2024** — "Proprioception and Control of a Soft Pneumatic Actuator Made of a Self-Healable Hydrogel," *Soft Robotics* 11(5):889–901. DOI 10.1089/soro.2023.0180. **S.** Actuator structure is the sensor; closed-loop on undamaged + healed actuators.
- **Lei et al. 2025** — "Condition monitoring and fault diagnosis of industrial robots: A review," *Sci. China Tech. Sci.* 68(1):1110301. DOI 10.1007/s11431-024-2810-2. **S.** Situates soft-robot prognostics in the broader (rigid) robot CBM literature; highlights the soft gap.
- **Legrand, Abdulali, Hardman, Terryn, Vanderborght, Iida 2023** — "Autonomous Testing of the Repeatable Healability of Pneumatic Self-Healing Soft Actuators," *Robotics Reports* 1(1). DOI 10.1089/rorep.2023.0014. **S.** Automated degradation/recovery tracking precedent.

## 3. Pressure-only / self-sensing proprioception (the A04 core)

- **L. Wang & Z. Wang 2020** — "Mechanoreception for Soft Robots via Intuitive Body Cues," *Soft Robotics* 7(2):198–217. DOI 10.1089/soro.2018.0135. **PRESSURE-ONLY · F.** The canonical pressure-only mechanoreception reference (Boyle's-law position, inter-chamber pressure-differential momentum observer for contact). Closest conceptual root.
- **L. Wang et al. 2023** — "Soft Robot Proprioception Using Unified Soft Body Encoding and RNN," *Soft Robotics* 10(4):825–837. DOI 10.1089/soro.2021.0056. **PRESSURE-ONLY · CPA.** Multi-chamber, pressure-only, redundant "receptors" + RNN; studies graceful degradation. Nearest neighbor to your shared-manifold setup — but assumes independent chambers.
- **J. Wang et al. 2025** — "Proprioceptive and Exteroceptive Information Perception in a Fabric Soft Robotic Arm via Physical Reservoir Computing…," *Adv. Intell. Syst.* 7(4):2400534 (arXiv:2411.07309). DOI 10.1002/aisy.202400534. **PRESSURE-ONLY · CPA/M.** Internal pressure → bending angle + payload via PRC linear readout.
- **Joshi & Paik 2023** — "Sensorless force and displacement estimation in soft actuators," *Soft Matter* 19. DOI 10.1039/D2SM01197B. **PRESSURE-ONLY · CPA/M.** Bijective **pressure–volume–force–displacement** mapping (pressure-oscillation trick to recover volume). Directly adjacent to your P-V thesis.
- **Zou et al. 2024** — "A retrofit sensing strategy for soft fluidic robots," *Nature Communications* 15:539. DOI 10.1038/s41467-023-44517-z. **PRESSURE-ONLY · CPA.** Infers deformed/contact state from the *fluidic input required to actuate* — but assumes a clean per-actuator input (no shared-manifold cross-talk).
- **Preechayasomboon & Rombokas 2021** — "Sensuator: A Hybrid Sensor–Actuator Approach to Soft Robotic Proprioception Using RNNs," *Actuators* 10(2):30. DOI 10.3390/act10020030. **PRESSURE-ONLY · CPA.** Actuating fluid as the sensing medium; multi-channel pressure → 6-DoF pose via RNN.
- **Qiao, Nguyen, Zhang 2022** — "Model-based contact detection and position control of a fabric soft robot…," *Front. Robot. AI* 9:997366. DOI 10.3389/frobt.2022.997366. **PRESSURE-ONLY · M.** Nonlinear disturbance observer estimates contact from pressure+position (model-based contrast to learning-based).
- **Jing et al. 2024** — "Self-Sensing for Proprioception and Contact Detection in Soft Robots Using SMA Artificial Muscles," arXiv:2409.17111. **SELF-SENSING (electrical, not pressure) · S.** Broadens "sense from signals already in the loop"; also reports **contact detection via precision/recall/F1** (metric reference).
- *Added-sensor distinction set (cite as contrast — NOT pressure-only):* **Thuruthel et al. 2019** (embedded soft sensors + RNN, *Sci. Robotics* 4(26):eaav1488, DOI 10.1126/scirobotics.aav1488, **F**); **Truby, Della Santina, Rus 2020** (sensorized skin, RA-L 5(2):3299–3306, DOI 10.1109/LRA.2020.2976320, **F**); **Scharff et al. 2020** (in-chamber sensors, arXiv:2012.12411, **S**); **Ibrahim et al. 2021** (IMU+pressure PCC, *Front. Robot. AI* 8:557830, DOI 10.3389/frobt.2021.557830, **S** — motivates *why pressure-only is hard*); **Yoo et al. 2024** (acoustic, arXiv:2401.09382, **S**).

## 4. Shared-manifold pneumatic coupling, cross-talk & fluidic-circuit modeling (the novel angle)

- **Lindenroth, Stoyanov, Rhode, Liu 2023** — "Toward Intrinsic Force Sensing and Control in Parallel Soft Robots," *IEEE/ASME Transactions on Mechatronics* 28(1):80–91. DOI 10.1109/TMECH.2022.3210065 (arXiv:2111.10338). **CPA — nearest collision.** Estimates contact forces from pressure+volume on **coupled parallel** chambers. The closest paper to your premise — but does *not* frame inter-chamber cross-talk as a proprioception confound or link it to fatigue.
- **Moran et al. 2024** — "An electropermanent magnet valve for the onboard control of multi-DoF pneumatic soft robots," *Comms. Eng.* 3:117. DOI 10.1038/s44172-024-00251-y. **CPA (hardware).** Three valves **in parallel on a single shared pressure source** — exactly your shared-manifold topology.
- **Bartlett, Becker, Wood 2020** — "A fluidic demultiplexer for controlling large arrays of soft actuators," *Soft Matter* 16(25). DOI 10.1039/C9SM02502B. **CPA (valve reduction).** n inputs → 2ⁿ outputs from one supply.
- **McDonald & Ranzani 2021** — "Hardware Methods for Onboard Control of Fluidically Actuated Soft Robots," *Front. Robot. AI* 8:720702. DOI 10.3389/frobt.2021.720702. **F (survey).** Best single survey for the shared-supply / valve-architecture background.
- **S. Wang et al. 2024** — "Toward Onboard Proportional Control of Multi-Chamber Soft Pneumatic Robots: An MRE Valve Array," *Soft Robotics* 11(4):617–627. DOI 10.1089/soro.2023.0049. **S.** Valve-count packing causes interference/overheating → motivates sharing supply.
- **Hoang et al. 2021** — "A pneumatic random-access memory for controlling soft robots," *PLoS ONE* 16(7):e0254524. DOI 10.1371/journal.pone.0254524. **S.** Time-division multiplexing of a shared source.
- **Stanley, Amini, Glick, Usevitch, Mengüç, Keller 2021** — "Lumped-Parameter Response Time Models for Pneumatic Circuit Dynamics," *ASME Journal of Dynamic Systems, Measurement, and Control* 143(5):051001. DOI 10.1115/1.4049009. **F (pneumatic modeling).** Canonical lumped resistance–capacitance (compliance) framework — the tool to *model* cross-talk as a function of chamber compliance.
- **Wang, Zhang, Zhu 2019** — "A computationally efficient dynamical model of fluidic soft actuators…," *Mechatronics* 56:1–8. DOI 10.1016/j.mechatronics.2018.11.012. **M.** Lumped 2nd-order pressure dynamics.
- **Zhou et al. 2021** — "Multistage Digital-to-Analogue Chip Based on a Weighted Flow Resistance Network for Soft Actuators," *Micromachines* 12(9):1016. DOI 10.3390/mi12091016. **M.** Flow-resistance network sets pressure distribution from one source.
- *Fluidic-logic context:* **Drotman et al. 2021** (*Sci. Robotics* 6(51):eaay2627, DOI 10.1126/scirobotics.aay2627, **S**); **Rothemund et al. 2018** (soft bistable valve, *Sci. Robotics* 3(16):eaar7986, DOI 10.1126/scirobotics.aar7986, **F**).

## 6. Experimental methodology — ground truth, pose definition, evaluation

- **Webster & Jones 2010** — "Design and Kinematic Modeling of Constant Curvature Continuum Robots: A Review," *IJRR* 29(13):1661–1683. DOI 10.1177/0278364910368147. **F.** Defines piecewise-constant-curvature (PCC) — what "curvature pose" formally means; cite when motivating tip-SE(3) vs. curvature.
- **Zhang, Wang, Truby, Chin, Rus 2023** — "Machine Learning Best Practices for Soft Robot Proprioception," *IROS 2023*:2564–2571. DOI 10.1109/IROS55552.2023.10342379. **F (eval standards).** Signal selection, normalization, time-series handling, architecture effects on pose error — your reporting-standards backbone.
- **Yoo et al. 2023** — "Toward Zero-Shot Sim-to-Real Transfer Learning for Pneumatic Soft Robot 3D Proprioceptive Sensing," *ICRA 2023*, DOI 10.1109/ICRA48891.2023.10160384 (arXiv:2303.04307). **M.** Concrete pneumatic metrics: Chamfer ~8.85 mm, tip ~10.12 mm.
- **Thuruthel et al. 2019** (above) — the reference template for *mocap-as-ground-truth* for a soft body; explicitly calls markers "a proxy representation."
- **Wang et al. 2020** — "Real-Time Soft Body 3D Proprioception via Deep Vision-Based Sensing," *RA-L* 5(2):3382–3389. DOI 10.1109/LRA.2020.2975709 (arXiv:1904.03820). **M.** RGBD dense-shape ground truth (occlusion-free contrast to markers).
- **Baaij et al. 2023** — "Learning 3D shape proprioception for continuum soft robots with multiple magnetic sensors," *Soft Matter* 19(1):44–56. DOI 10.1039/D2SM00914E. **S.** Kinematic-prior network for data efficiency.
- **Monet et al. 2020** — "High-Resolution Optical Fiber Shape Sensing of Continuum Robots: A Comparative Study," *ICRA 2020*:8877–8883. DOI 10.1109/ICRA40945.2020.9197454. **M.** Curvature-from-strain degrades on S-bends/multi-inflection → supports tip-SE(3) primary truth.
- **An, Cui, Dong, Wang, Du, Liu, Zhao 2024** — "Shape reconstruction of soft continuum robots via the fusion of local strains and global poses," *Cell Reports Physical Science* 5(10):102224. DOI 10.1016/j.xcrp.2024.102224. **M.** Strain-only reconstruction accumulates error along length → fuse with global poses.
- **Shih et al. 2020** — "Electronic skins and machine learning for intelligent soft robots," *Sci. Robotics* 5(41):eaaz9239. DOI 10.1126/scirobotics.aaz9239. **F.** Field-level framing of mapping drifting high-dim signals to a pose state.

**Ground-truth recommendation (synthesized):** measure **fingertip SE(3) with a small rigid marker cluster on the least-deforming distal boss** as primary truth; treat curvature/PCC as a *modeled output validated against* that truth, not as measured truth — this sidesteps the marker-shear-on-silicone and curvature-error-accumulation problems the literature repeatedly flags. Metrics: pose RMSE (mm + deg), contact precision/recall/F1, Chamfer distance only if a dense reconstruction is claimed.
